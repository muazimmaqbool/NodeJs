->Authentication (verifying identity):
  Authentication is the process of verifying who a user is.

  Example:
  When you log in with your username and password, the system checks if you are really who you say you are.

->Authorization (access control):
  Authorization is the process of checking what a user is allowed to do.

  Example:
  After you log in, the system checks whether you are allowed to access a specific page, file, or action (like admin panel or deleting users).
